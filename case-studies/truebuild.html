The Problem

TrueBuild’s website was built for human visitors and traditional search. It had no structured identity files, no AI-readable context, and no mechanism for AI assistants to understand what TrueBuild does, who it serves, or how to accurately describe it.

As AI-powered search and AI assistants become primary discovery channels, businesses without structured context risk being misrepresented, overlooked, or simply invisible to AI systems.

The AFO Package

  • ✓llms.txt — plain-text LLM identity summary
  • ✓agent-context.json — structured business identity
  • ✓agent-actions.json — available actions for AI agents
  • ✓agent-policy.json — compliance and claim boundaries
  • ✓context-cookie.json — concise identity memory object
  • ✓sitemap-agent.xml — agent-optimized sitemap
  • ✓robots.txt — AI-annotated crawler permissions
  • ✓Before/after audit JSON — benchmarkable visibility data
  • ✓Proof report — structured visibility record
  • ✓JSON-LD schema markup — structured page identity
